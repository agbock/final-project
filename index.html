About Us

We are a Harrisonburg-based event & floral design studio that specializes in earth friendly + nature-inspired floral design & custom installations for events, weddings, & more. Our goal is to create, design, and collaborate with you and your other vendors in an easy, fun, and professional manner to bring your vision to life. We believe that flowers are magic and that they have the power to transform spaces, people, and bad days alike. Flowers are a powerful reminder to be present, and they teach us to cultivate a more mindful relationship with ourselves and the world around us.
